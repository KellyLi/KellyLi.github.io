As a product designer with an engineering background, I believe that we should never sacrifice beauty or function, and instead should be looking for where they intersect. From microinteractions and behaviour modelling, to speed and code quality-- the interactions between all system components determine the success of a product. It's important to be a holistic designer, and I've been challenging myself on every aspect from product design, technical development, to visual execution.

I work in Photoshop, Illustrator, Vim, and Framer. Fluent in Python, HTML, CSS, and JS.
